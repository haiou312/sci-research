## Story Rules

- Use a concise factual headline.
- Keep `摘要` to one compact paragraph.
- `关键影响` explains what changes for Chinese enterprises, the UK/European market, or the transaction.
- `商机切入点` states plausible banking products and the evidence-based reason to investigate them. Use cautious language.
- `后续关注` names the next filing, approval, financing, earnings release, construction milestone, policy event, or relationship action.
- Include one primary source and any necessary corroborating source.
- Use an eligible image plus caption/source, or the exact no-image line from `image-policy.md`.
- Never combine unrelated events under one headline.

## Institutional Layout Rules

- The first page must expose report status, period, three to five conclusions,
  and the opportunity priority table before detailed stories.
- Follow the analytical sequence `事实` → `影响` → `商机假设` → `触发点`.
- Use restrained SPD Bank blue with limited red accents for high priority and
  internal classification.
- Prefer one decision-useful image per major theme; do not add decorative stock
  imagery.
- Keep source names and URLs visible in the DOCX.
- Keep the normal edition compact enough to scan without a contents page.
- Put methodology, coverage limits, and the disclaimer at the end.

## Opportunity Table

- Include 3-6 highest-priority actions.
- Use `高`, `中`, or `观察`.
- Keep each cell concise enough for Word table rendering.
- `建议动作` should identify a concrete internal follow-up such as contacting a named company, checking group coverage, preparing a sector note, or verifying financing status.

## Companies House Table

- Include only `confirmed` and carefully labelled `probable` entities.
- Render confidence as `已确认` or `高概率`; never use `已确认` for indirect evidence.
- Always include company number and the filing/effective date within `本期变化`.
- Explain API/watchlist limitations in `Companies House 覆盖说明`.

## Tone

- Write in Simplified Chinese for senior institutional readers.
- Lead with the development, not the date or source.
- Use short paragraphs and dense factual clauses.
- Separate fact from inference.
- Avoid promotional language, generic conclusions, and unsupported certainty.

## Disclaimer

Use:

```text
免责声明：本简报基于公开资料编制，仅用于内部商业情报和客户拓展研判。公开登记信息可能存在申报时滞、错误或不完整，Companies House 记录亦不等同于对最终控制关系的独立确认。文中商机切入点属于基于公开事实的初步判断，不代表相关企业已提出融资、交易或银行服务需求，也不构成投资、法律、税务或信贷建议。
```
