# Image Policy

## Objective

Prefer one informative image or chart for a major story when it improves comprehension. Evidence quality and relevance take priority over decoration.

## Eligible for Embedding

- official government, regulator, statistics-agency, or central-bank charts;
- official company press or investor-relations images directly connected to the event;
- charts generated from cited public data;
- licensed media images when the licence or distribution right is clear.

## Link Only

Use a source link without embedding when the image is:

- from ordinary news-media photography without clear reuse permission;
- behind access controls;
- available only as a thumbnail or unstable social-media URL;
- marked with unclear or unknown rights.

## Prohibited

- fabricated documentary or news images;
- AI-generated depictions presented as actual events, companies, factories, executives, or transactions;
- unrelated stock photography;
- logos stretched as decorative hero images;
- images without a source.

## Required Metadata

For every embedded image capture:

- direct or retrievable URL;
- caption describing what the image shows;
- image owner/source;
- rights note from the Scanner schema;
- associated story ID.

If no eligible image is available, write:

```text
**配图：** 未采用（未找到可合法嵌入且与事件直接相关的权威图片）
```

Do not lower story priority because no eligible image exists.
